\begin{abstract}
Safe navigation under RGB-D perception noise remains a critical challenge for low-cost mobile robots deployed in unstructured indoor environments. We present a LeRobot-compatible residual safety navigation framework for the LeKiwi omnidirectional mobile platform equipped with an Intel RealSense D435i camera. [Full abstract in Markdown draft.]
\end{abstract}
